This project, Multidisciplinary Design: Bridging Disciplines, Shaping Ideas, reimagines an Are.na collection as an interactive interface that visualizes the interconnected nature of design disciplines.

Typography

Cormorant Garamond for elegance and depth

Roboto Mono for clarity and structure

Concept

Inspired by interlocking loops, the interface reflects how disciplines intersect, evolve, and influence one another. A color-coded system highlights different media types, reinforcing the idea of fluid, cross-disciplinary connections.
